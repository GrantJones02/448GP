White and Black Pieces

You will notice on a chessboard
arranged in its starting position
has two colors of pieces:
Black and White.

The two colors of pieces correspond
to the two players of the game,
and each player has the same
set of pieces to use.

The player with the white pieces
makes the first move.

Pawns

Pawns are the weakest and most numerous
piece in your arsenal.

They are the smallest piece on the board
and are have a rounded face with no detail.

The second and seventh rows
contain the pawns.

piece in your arsenal.

She has a crown atop her head,
